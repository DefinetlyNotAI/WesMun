About WESMUN


WESMUN (Wesgreen Model United Nations) is an annual, student-led event organized by Wesgreen International School in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates.

Replacing the official Model United Nations, this conference provides a platform for students to represent countries and collaborate on solving some of the world’s most pressing issues over the span of three days. The event enhances key skills such as public speaking, critical thinking, diplomacy, and teamwork.
