Letter from the Director

Dear International Students, Faculty and Institutions, 


It is my utmost pleasure to invite your esteemed institution to participate in the 18th Annual Conference of the American University of Sharjah Model United Nations (AUSMUN) 2025. My name is Iryn Tony, and as the Director of External Relations for AUSMUN 2025, I am honored to extend this invitation on behalf of the Secretary-General and the entire Executive Board.


AUSMUN is more than just a conference—it is a vibrant platform for meaningful dialogue, collaboration, and the exchange of innovative ideas. Bringing together over 1,200 delegates from diverse backgrounds, we believe that every voice adds invaluable depth to our discussions, and the perspectives of your students will play a crucial role in shaping the outcomes of our debates. 

Our team has designed an environment that is both intellectually stimulating and welcoming, with sessions, workshops, and events tailored to enhance your experience and foster meaningful exchanges. Beyond the formal proceedings, we hope you take advantage of the cultural richness that Sharjah has to offer. From its stunning architecture and museums to its vibrant arts scene, Sharjah provides a unique backdrop for our deliberations.

As the Director of External Relations, I am here to ensure that your experience at AUSMUN is as smooth and rewarding as possible. My role is to be your point of contact, to assist with any logistical needs, and to provide you with all the information you may require before, during, and after the conference. Whether you need help navigating the venue, understanding the agenda, or simply want to discuss the finer points of a resolution, please do not hesitate to reach out. I am here to support you every step of the way

Your presence is the key to our collective success, and we are excited to see the positive impact you will make. Once again, we are thrilled to welcome you at AUSMUN 2025 and look forward to a conference filled with meaningful exchanges, powerful resolutions, and enduring memories.

Best regards, 

Iryn Tony 

Director of External Relations
