THE EIGHTEENTH SESSION

American University of Sharjah

Model United Nations

February 21st, 2025 — February 23rd, 2025

“Envisioning Union: Guided by Dialogue and Diplomacy”

Letter from the Secretary-General

Dear Delegates, Advisors, and Organizers 

With utmost pleasure, I welcome you to the 18th edition of the American University of Sharjah’s Model United Nations (AUSMUN). As the Secretary-General, I look forward to joining you on this exciting journey.

AUSMUN is the region’s largest and most prestigious Model United Nations conference. It has always been a platform where delegates from diverse backgrounds debate issues that concern the international community and showcase or improve their speaking, negotiation, writing, etc. skills. This year, I and my executive board ensure to continue the legacy of the conference with the highest dedication.

This year's theme, "Envisioning Union: Guided by Dialogue and Diplomacy," speaks to the demands of the contemporary global environment. Globally, there is a growing need for dialogue and diplomacy as rifts deepen, which this year’s conference aims to provide to the next generation for the prevalence of world peace.

With “Sapere Aude” as our guiding light, I encourage you to participate in AUSMUN for a knowledge-driven, enjoyable, and memorable experience.

Warm Regards,

Sarvagya Sharma

Secretary-General
