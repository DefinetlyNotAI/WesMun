About the Committee

The United Nations Security Council Junior (UNSC Jr) is a simulation of the United Nations Security Council designed for younger or less experienced participants in Model United Nations (MUN) conferences. It mirrors the procedures and responsibilities of the actual UNSC but is tailored to be more accessible and educational. In UNSC Jr, delegates represent countries and discuss global issues, making decisions on conflict resolution, peacekeeping missions, and sanctions. It provides a platform for students to learn about international diplomacy, critical thinking, and teamwork while engaging with real-world issues.
