Welcome to WesMun

Wesgreen's own MUN

The first Of its Kind

Welcome to Wesgreen's International School very own MUN, Where Students compete with each other to become the #1 Coder.
Are you Up for the challenge?

The WesMun 25 is split into 2 categories, the Junior Category for students from years 7-9 and the Senior Category for students from years 10-12. Seniors and Juniors are both in the same prize pool, but some leniency goes to the Juniors. Juniors are allowed to program using block-based or hybrid languages, while Seniors are only allowed to use text-based languages.
